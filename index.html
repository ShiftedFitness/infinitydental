01 — Services

Complete dental care, under one roof

From a sudden toothache at midnight to a full smile makeover — every treatment is planned and delivered by Dr Maryam’s team.

24/7

Emergency dentistry

Same-day appointments for pain, trauma, lost fillings and swelling. Relief first, plan second.

Learn more

Acute toothache, an injured or knocked-out tooth, or facial swelling — call or WhatsApp the 24/7/365 emergency line. More severe cases are prioritised; the first 60 minutes are critical for a knocked-out tooth.

Replace a knocked-out adult tooth in its socket if possible, or store it in saline, milk or saliva and come straight in. We will guide you over the phone before you arrive.

Cosmetic dentistry

Veneers, whitening and smile design — natural results, no Hollywood exaggeration.

Learn more

Every procedure under the cosmetic-dentistry umbrella — professional whitening, porcelain veneers, composite bonding and complete smile design.

Dr Maryam plans every case with you and we examine the gums and supporting tissues first to make sure the treatment can be done safely.

Lifetime Whitening Program: at every routine check-up you receive professional at-home whitening gel to keep your smile bright between visits.

Aesthetic & Botox

Facial aesthetics performed by a dentist: Botox for bruxism and gummy smile, and dermal filler for harmonious lip and smile lines.

Learn more

Facial aesthetics performed by a dentist — Dr Maryam treats the muscles and soft tissues that frame your smile.

Botox for bruxism and gummy smile. Hyaluronic-acid filler for harmonious lip and smile lines. Subtle, reversible and always in proportion — Marbella, not Hollywood.

Dental implants

Single implants to full-arch All-on-4. Guided surgery, CT-planned, lifetime guarantees.

Learn more

The only way to replace a missing tooth completely — including the root. A titanium screw fuses with the jawbone and stimulates new bone growth, preventing the bone loss that always follows a missing tooth.

Dr Maryam has placed more than 2,000 implants over 25+ years. Every case is CT-planned and surgically guided. Single implant + crown, implant-supported bridge, or All-on-4 / full-arch.

Orthodontics

Invisalign certified provider. Clear aligners for teens and adults.

Learn more

Invisalign — clear, removable aligners planned digitally from a 3D scan; each set is worn for around two weeks.

Fastbraces® — addresses the same bad bites as conventional braces (overbite, underbite, crowding, gaps, open bite, crossbite) but in a single technical step. Treatment commonly completes in around 120 days, with less reported pain than traditional braces.

General & preventive

Check-ups, hygiene, fillings and root canals — the maintenance that keeps you out of the chair.

Learn more

The best dentistry is the dentistry you don't notice. A regular check-up and hygiene routine prevents cavities, gum disease and bone loss before any of them become a problem. It is cheaper to take care of something than to replace it.

Includes routine check-ups and X-rays, professional hygiene, white fillings, root-canal treatment, atraumatic extractions and night guards. 20% discount on services for elderly patients.

Paediatric dentistry

Gentle, fear-free care for children from age 2, in English or Spanish.

Learn more

Children's first dental visits set the tone for life — so we keep them gentle, fear-free and short.

From age two, light friendly check-ups in English or Spanish: a tour, count-the-teeth game, gentle cleaning, fluoride if appropriate. Parents always welcome in the room. If treatment is needed, we explain it to the child first, then to you.

02 — Emergency

What counts as a dental emergency?

We are available for emergencies 24/7/365. To guarantee your slot, please call or WhatsApp first — we can normally see you within an hour.

Severe toothache

Throbbing pain, pain waking you at night, or pain that paracetamol will not touch.

Come in now

Knocked-out tooth

Adult tooth knocked out? The first hour is critical — keep it moist and come in immediately.

Come in now

Broken or cracked tooth

A visible crack, a chipped front tooth, or sharp edges cutting your tongue.

Come in now

Abscess or facial swelling

Swelling of the gum, cheek or jaw — potentially serious. Do not wait.

Come in now

Lost filling or crown

Something fell out while you were eating? We can refit or replace it, usually same-day.

Come in now

Bleeding that will not stop

Gum bleeding after trauma or a procedure that continues after 20 minutes of pressure.

Come in now
